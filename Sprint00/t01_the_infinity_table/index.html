The Infinity Stones

Name Color Powers and capabilities Image
Soul Orange The Soul Stone is said to be the most mysterious and dangerous of all the Infinity Stones. It has the ability to manipulate and control living and dead souls. It grants its wielder access to all life in the universe, enabling them to manipulate and control souls, including resurrecting the dead or trapping souls within the Soul World.
Time Green The Time Stone allows its wielder to manipulate time itself. It grants powers such as time travel, time loops, and the ability to see into the future or past. It is contained within the Eye of Agamotto and has been used by Doctor Strange to manipulate time during battles and foresee future events.
Space Blue The Space Stone has the power to manipulate space, allowing its wielder to teleport themselves or others to any location in the universe instantly. It can create wormholes for interstellar travel and distort space, making objects appear larger or smaller than they are. It is contained within the Tesseract and has been used by various characters to travel across galaxies.
Mind Yellow The Mind Stone grants its wielder immense mental powers, including telepathy, mind control, and the ability to enhance intelligence and cognitive functions. It was originally housed in Loki's scepter and has been used by characters like Vision to gain insight into others' thoughts and control their actions.
Reality Red The Reality Stone has the ability to warp and manipulate reality itself. It can alter the laws of physics, create illusions, and change the very fabric of existence. It can create alternate realities, reshape matter, and transform one object into another. It appears as a red, viscous liquid known as the Aether and has been sought after by powerful beings seeking to reshape the universe.
Power Purple The Power Stone is a source of immense energy, capable of enhancing the physical strength and abilities of its wielder to god-like levels. It can also be used to unleash devastating energy blasts and destroy entire planets. The Power Stone grants its wielder incredible power and has been sought after by beings seeking to dominate the universe through force.
