Superhero trivia

What makes a superhero?

In a world filled with ordinary people, superheroes stand out as extraordinary individuals with exceptional abilities. They often possess superhuman strength, speed, agility, and other remarkable powers. These abilities are used to protect the innocent, fight crime, and uphold justice. One of the most iconic superheroes is Flash, known for his incredible speed and quick thinking. Learn more about Flash here.
Flash is a founding member of the Justice League, fighting alongside other superheroes like Superman, Batman, and Wonder Woman.

My favorite superhero

Flash, also known as Barry Allen, is a forensic scientist who gained his super speed after being struck by lightning and doused in chemicals. Since then, he has used his powers to fight crime and protect the innocent as the Scarlet Speedster.
